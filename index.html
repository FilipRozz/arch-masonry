What We Do

Skilled masonry,
built to last.

Arch Masonry is a fully qualified stonemasons company based in Bristol. Our expert team delivers a full range of masonry services to residential and commercial clients across Bristol and the South West, with a 100% satisfaction guarantee.

Stone Masonry

Expert stonework crafted with precision. From decorative features to structural masonry, our experience guarantees top-level results every time.

Repointing

Specialist repointing to restore joints and protect your stonework from water ingress. We match mortar mixes carefully to your existing stone.

New Build

Full new-build masonry for residential and commercial properties. On time, on budget, with no compromise on quality.

Stone Walling

Rubble stone, coursed, dry stone — we build walls that are structurally sound and visually stunning using traditional techniques.

Stone Cleaning

Safe, professional cleaning that removes dirt and staining without damaging your stone. We use the right method for every surface.

Restoration

Bringing old stone back to life. We restore historic stonework sympathetically, preserving character and ensuring structural integrity.
